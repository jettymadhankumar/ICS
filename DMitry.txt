DMitry: (Deep Magic Information Gathering Tool)
-------
* It is a Linux based command line appication coded in "C Language".

* It will gather the information about a host.

* Specifically: sub domains, email addresses, uptime, TCP port scan, etc.

* options: -i, -w, -s, -e, -p, -o

* syntax: dmitry [options] [IP address/domain]

* Ex:	
	1. dmitry -i becbapatla.ac.in
	2. dmitry -w facebook.com
	3. dmitry  -s alexa.com
	4. dmitry  -p 192.168.71.128
	5. dmitry  -pf google.com
	6. dmitry  -e facebook.com
	7. dmitry -n alexa.com
	8. dmitry -iweso /root/Desktop/info.txt becbapatla.ac.in
